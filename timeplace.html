Importance of Internship

Internship is an essential part of being a student in Information Technology because it marks the shift from academic learning to real-world experience. While school teaches us the theories and concepts, internship allows us to apply them in actual situations. It introduces us to real challenges that cannot be fully learned through books alone.

This experience does not only improve my technical skills but also helps shape my character. I learn how to collaborate with others, manage my time effectively, communicate professionally, and handle pressure in the workplace. It also helps me discover which area of IT I am most interested in, whether it is development, networking, or system support.

Overall, internships contribute to both my personal and professional growth. They give me a clearer understanding of what working in the industry is like and prepare me to face future responsibilities with greater confidence and direction after graduation.

Objectives of Internship

The internship aims to provide students with hands-on experience and enhance their competencies in the field of Information Technology, particularly in the Billing Office of Occidental Mindoro Electric Cooperative (OMECO). Specifically, it seeks to:

1.Ensure that students acquire the necessary knowledge, technical skills, and competencies related to billing operations, including data encoding, record management, and the use of computerized billing systems.

2.Improve students’ problem-solving and analytical skills in handling billing concerns, such as verifying consumer accounts, identifying discrepancies, and assisting in the accurate computation of charges.

3.Enhance skills in office and strengthen the ability to efficiently process billing records, generate reports, and maintain organized files.

4.Engage students in actual office tasks within a professional work environment, and prepare them for future careers by exposing them to real workplace practices, standards, and customer service interactions.

5.Encourage proper coordination with supervisors and adherence to company rules and procedures, while developing discipline through punctuality, responsibility, teamwork, and consistent performance.

Time and Place of Internship

Time of practicum: Monday–Friday (8:00 AM – 12:00 NN and 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM)
