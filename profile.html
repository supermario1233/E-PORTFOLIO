COMPANY PROFILE

Nature of the Agency

The Occidental Mindoro Electric Cooperative (OMECO) is a non-stock, non-profit electric distribution utility located in San Jose, Occidental Mindoro. It is responsible for providing reliable and efficient electricity services to the residents and businesses within its coverage area in the province.

OMECO operates under the supervision and regulation of the National Electrification Administration (NEA) and is committed to delivering quality service to its member-consumers. The cooperative plays a vital role in supporting economic growth and community development by ensuring a stable supply of electricity.

Since its establishment, OMECO has continuously worked to improve its operations, maintain its facilities, and adopt modern systems to better serve the public. It also promotes customer satisfaction, accountability, and sustainability in all its services, aiming to meet the increasing demand for electricity while contributing to the overall progress of Occidental Mindoro.

Mission

A distribution utility delivering service in a socially responsible manner for the improvement of the quality of life of the people in mainland Occidental Mindoro.

Vision

To be a financially stable electric cooperative providing quality, reliable, and sustainable electric service using world-class systems operated by competent personnel.

History of the Agency

In 1969, President Decree No. 269 created the National Electrification Administration which was charged with the responsibility of organizing, financing and regulating electric cooperatives throughout the country and attaining the declared goal of the government- the total electrification of all municipalities by the year 1987.

Through the initiative of Atty. Eufrocino L. Ramos, head of the Provincial Electrification Cooperative team (PECT), the Occidental Mindoro Electric Cooperative, Inc. (OMECO) was organized in 1974. An initial loan of P6.9 Million was signed in March 1974 between NEA officials and the members of the interim Board of Directors of OMECO headed by Dr. Rodrigo M. David.
