Appendix O • Internship Guidelines

College of Arts, Sciences, and Technology

Student Internship Code of Ethics

Official code of ethics that governed student conduct during the internship.

Preamble

I will use my special knowledge for the benefit of the public. I will serve employees and clients with integrity, subject to an overriding responsibility to the public interest, and I will strive to enhance the competence and prestige of the profession.

Professional Responsibilities
  • I will promote public knowledge, understanding, and appreciation of information technology.
  • I will consider the general welfare and public good in the performance of my work.
  • I will advertise goods or professional services in a clear and truthful manner.
  • I will comply strictly with intellectual property laws, patent laws, and related IT regulations.
  • I will accept full responsibility for my work and apply skills with competence and professionalism.
  • I will provide truthful statements regarding my capabilities and professional competence.
  • I will not disclose confidential information without proper consent, except when required by law.
  • I will strive to achieve the highest quality in all products and services I deliver.
  • I will not participate in systems that promote fraud or illegal activities.
  • I will continuously improve my IT skills and uphold the standards of the profession.
